What is a Class Designer?

A Class Designer is in charge of defining, implementing, and maintaining the Diablo IV playable classes all while ensuring they are engaging and balanced. These classes have unique strengths, weaknesses, identities, and core fantasies that are expressed through the skills, items, and seasonal powers that Class Designers create.
