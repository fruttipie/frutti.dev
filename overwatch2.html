What is a Hero Designer?

In Overwatch, a Hero Designer creates the gameplay mechanics and abilities for the many playable heroes. An Overwatch hero typically has inherent parameters such as Health and Ammo, 4 to 6 active abilities, in-match progression through Perks and Stadium powers and hero items, a passive ability, and one game-changing ultimate ability. Every Overwatch hero has situations that allow the player's skills to shine and others that intentionally create challenges.

Who is ScrapMouse?

ScrapMouse is an Overwatch hero that I created during my 2023 Hero Design Internship at Blizzard. She is a medium-range DPS hero who wields a homemade nail gun with a rebar launcher on top. My intended player fantasy was that of a nimble, scrappy hero whose power snowballs as she continues to do more damage.
She is also a chaos-loving gremlin who named herself after Junkrat
(Junk = Scrap, Rat = Mouse ... get it?).
