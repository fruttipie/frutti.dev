My Design Process!

1. Understand Goals

Speak with fellow Designers and other departments/disciplines (example: Narrative Designers) to understand goals and constraints.

2. Brainstorm & Research

What could be missing from our game? What similar content already exists in our game or others? What did players think of that content?

3. Implement

Implement, iterate, and debug prototypes by scripting and/or working in-editor.

4. Playtest

Lead and participate in playtests to give and receive valuable feedback and data.
Unless things feel "just right," iterate!
